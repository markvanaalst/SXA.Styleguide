Sitecore Experience Accelerator

SXA is a platform and a concept that enables organizations to perform quick delivery of sites, using standardized sets of functionality with minimum-to-no CMS development. For the purpose of achieving this goal, Sitecore Experience Accelerator utilizes a number of concepts, like grid frameworks and a componentized architecture, to allow for easy and consistent styling of pages and ease of design.

Lower time to market & increase productivity

Every feature of SXA comes with productivity in mind - it is meant to be an expert tool that enables the delivery of websites quickly with minimum custom development. This, however, might mean something different for different people - that is why SXA defines clearly its target audiences and plans its roadmap with an acute awareness of the clients.
With multiple markets and multi-lingual support, it is easy to spread the word about your greatness to the customers who speak in all languages.
